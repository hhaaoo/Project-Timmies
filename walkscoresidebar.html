Walk Score


Walk Score measures the walkability of any address based on the distances to nearby places and pedestrian friendliness

  • 90-100: Walker's paradise

    Daily errands do not require a car

  • 70-89: Very Walkable

    Most errands can be accomplished on foot

  • 50-69: Somewhat Walkable

    Most errands require a car

  • 25-49: Car Dependent

    Most errands require a car

  • 0-24: Car Dependent

    Almost all errands require a car
