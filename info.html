Basic Information:

  1. A lease is a legal contract between you and your landlord. It can be in writing, or it can be a spoken (oral/verbal) agreement.
  2. Is only able to ask for one month's rent as a deposit, which will be used as payment for your last month of tenancy.
  3. Can ask that you pay your first month's rent up front.
  4. There can be week-to-week, month to month and 12 month term leases.
  5. Reference is someone who can speak on your behalf to prove that you are a reliable tenant
  6. A Guarantor is someone that can guarantee that your rent will be paid every month and is financially liable to cover the rent, if you miss yours
  7. In Emergencies where you are in need of a place to stay for the night, please contact Campus Police at 416-978-2323
